# Code of Conduct

TLTE / VinMin is built on respect, responsibility, and constructive
collaboration.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:
- act with respect and professionalism
- communicate constructively and thoughtfully
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or hostile behaviour
- respect privacy and confidentiality
- follow applicable laws and platform rules

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Unacceptable behaviour includes:
- harassment or hate speech
- abuse or intimidation
- deliberate misinformation
- attempts to exploit or disrupt the platform
- sharing private or sensitive information without consent

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take appropriate action for violations, including:
- warnings
- content removal
- temporary or permanent restrictions

## Commitment

By participating in this repository, you agree to uphold these standards
and contribute to a safe and productive environment.

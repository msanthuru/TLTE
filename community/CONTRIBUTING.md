# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in contributing to TLTE / VinMin.

## How to Contribute

You may contribute by:
- suggesting improvements
- fixing bugs or documentation issues
- proposing enhancements
- sharing constructive feedback

## Guidelines

- keep changes focused and well-documented
- follow existing project structure and style
- do not submit sensitive or private material
- ensure all contributions are lawful and ethical
